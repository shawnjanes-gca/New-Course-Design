What Exactly Does “Asset Protection” Mean?

Asset Protection is simply the strategic use of perfectly legal means to insulate the net worth and future earnings of your clients from future creditors. Asset protection is a process of moving assets from a vulnerable ownership form to a protected ownership form. An effective asset protection plan can protect virtually any asset.

The adage “never bring a knife to a gunfight” could have been written to describe asset protection. Creditors have a “gun” in the huge body of law at their disposal to attack a debtor’s assets. Your clients need their own “gun” to fight liability claims; in other words, they need an effective plan that leverages plaintiff-friendly asset protection laws.
