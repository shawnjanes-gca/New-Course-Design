Legal Disclaimer

Asset protection laws vary from state to state and, at times, even among different courts within a state.

Example of Variations in State Laws

A majority of the individual states allow a married couple to use the Tenancy by the Entirety (TBE) form of property ownership. The lion’s share of those states allowing TBE only permit real property to be owned while a few states restrict TBE ownership to only a couple’s personal residence.1

Hence, competent legal counsel should always be consulted in the formation of an asset protection plan.

Legal Disclaimer

Nothing in this course is intended to serve as legal advice. The content in this course should not be used as legal advice. An attorney should ALWAYS be consulted when asset protection is discussed.

Engage your compliance team or an attorney to discuss the dividing line between delivering quality service to your clients and rendering legal advice. Rendering legal advice without a license to practice law is a criminal offense punishable by jail time and/or fine.
