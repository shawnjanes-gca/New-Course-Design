How an Irrevocable Life Insurance Trust Works

Here is how an irrevocable trust with Crummey powers works each year as contributions are made to make the premiums.

Important: Please note that contributions are made to the trust and the trustee makes the premium payments. It is very important that the grantor not send the premiums directly to the insurance company. Doing so might open the arrangement up to challenge by the IRS.
