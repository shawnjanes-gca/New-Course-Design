Summary

Hedge Funds can be a highly effective investment alternative for qualified participants seeking new strategies to increase the return potential and diversification in their portfolio. However, many investors do not fully understand what hedge funds are and how they can be used to achieve these goals. By mastering the topics covered in this course you will be equipped with the ability to discuss the merits of investing in hedge funds.
