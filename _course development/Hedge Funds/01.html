In recent years, hedge funds have grown dramatically in popularity, both with individual and institutional investors. This growth in popularity can be attributed to many different factors, including the growth of wealth among individuals in the last ten years, investor's ever-expanding desire for new investment alternatives and the need to further diversify their portfolio beyond traditional investment approaches. As such, it is vital that you as a financial advisor are empowered with a sound understanding of how hedge funds operate and the potential advantages they can provide. This course will equip you with a clear understanding of hedge funds and an enhanced ability to engage clients in a discussion and analysis about the potential efficacy of investing in hedge funds.
