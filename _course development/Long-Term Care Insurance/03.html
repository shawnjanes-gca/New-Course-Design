The Ultimate Risk – Insufficient Funds

Ultimately, the risk is that individuals will not have necessary resources to obtain the long-term care that they need and/or desire. On the following pages, we review the following types of long-term care providers:

  • Nursing Homes
  • Assisted Living Facilities
  • Alzheimer’s Facilities
  • Adult Day Care
  • Daily Money Managers
  • Home Health Care
For each, we shall provide estimates of usage in order to further illuminate the extent of the financial risk of long-term care.
