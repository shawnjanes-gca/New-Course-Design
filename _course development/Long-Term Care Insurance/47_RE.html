1.

All of the following are characteristics of individuals who will be more likely to be suitable purchasers of long-term care insurance EXCEPT:

Incorrect. Likely purchasers of LTCi have low risk tolerance.
Incorrect. Prospects for LTCi in their fifties and in good health can generally acquire LTCi at relatively low rates. People in this age range are generally becoming concerned about the security of their retirement years.
Incorrect. People with a family history of long-term care needs tend to be more concerned about the risk.
Correct! People with modest resources will often find premiums for LTCi difficult to maintain, especially after retirement.
