Introduction

Objectives

At the conclusion of this course, you will be able to:

Perceive insurance as a "solution" rather than a product.

Identify the risk factors people experience as they go through various stages of financial life.

Associate specific insurance solutions with the risk factors that are present in the various stages of the financial lifecycle.

Identify the primary insurance solutions being used today.

Understand the various life insurance policy structures.

Understand the various types of disability insurance.

Understand the range of insurance solutions that relate to the financial planning process for your business owner clients.

Gaining the requisite knowledge to advise clients regarding insurance needs can be an intimidating task. Many people who study insurance are simply overwhelmed by the complexity of various insurance products. This course will steer a path through that complexity, helping you easily assimilate the requisite knowledge. Its purpose is to provide you with a framework to better understand various insurance solutions - making you more capable of effectively positioning insurance as a financial tool to be used alongside other investment and estate planning tools in the financial planning process.
