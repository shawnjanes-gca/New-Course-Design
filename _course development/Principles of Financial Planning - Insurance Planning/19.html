Variable Universal Life Insurance

This type of insurance provides the features of both variable and universal life insurance.   Like universal life, the owner can choose the following:

  1. The premium amount
  2. The face amount
  3. Typically, the type of death benefit (fixed or variable)

But like variable life, the cash value is placed in a separate investment account(s) and the owner assumes all the investment risk. Also like variable life, only a licensed broker/dealer can sell this type of policy.
