The Three Types of Life Insurance

There are three basic types of life insurance: Term Life, Permanent Life, and Endowment.

Click each type to learn more.

 

Term Life Insurance

Provides a death benefit only and exists for a specific time period.

Permanent Life Insurance

Provides a lifetime death benefit and may provide a savings element that is reflected as "cash value."

Endowment Insurance

Provides a stated benefit that is payable upon a specific date. If the insured dies before that date, then it is payable to the insured's estate.
