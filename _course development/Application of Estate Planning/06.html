Estate Tax Deductions

  • The amount that can be transferred to a U.S. citizen spouse without tax is unlimited.

  • Transfers to charities are deductible.

A quick examination of the estate tax return reveals that there are two unlimited deductions available:

  • The unlimited marital deduction
  • The charitable deduction

No transfer tax is assessed against these transfers and none of the Applicable Credit/Exclusion needs to be applied on a gift tax return or estate tax return to protect these transfers from taxation.
