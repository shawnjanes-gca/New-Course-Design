How Much of a Traditional IRA Contribution is Deductible?

1.

A married couple, filing jointly, has modified AGI of $88,000 for 2016. Both are active participants in an employer-sponsored plan. The wife makes a $5,500 contribution to her Traditional IRA. How much of her contribution is deductible?

Correct. The phase-out for this situation takes place when modified AGI is between $98,000 and $118,000 (2016). Since their modified AGI is $88,000, her contributions are fully deductible..
