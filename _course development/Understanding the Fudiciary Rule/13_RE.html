1.

You are a Fiduciary Advisor to your client Ruth. Under the Grandfathered Exemption, you are permitted to receive variable compensation arising from which of the following IRA recommendations to Ruth? The phrase “pre-existing” in the answer choices below means “existing before April 10, 2017.”

Recommend, after April 10, 2017, an investment to Ruth that will be funded from the pre-existing IRA balance.

Incorrect. Variable compensation arising from recommendations made after the applicability date is not permitted by the GE (even if the investment is purchased with pre-existing funds).
Correct. Variable compensation arising from recommendations made after the applicability date is not permitted by the GE (even if the investment is purchased by pre-existing funds).

Recommend, after April 10, 2017, an investment to Ruth that will be funded by a contribution to her IRA made after April 10, 2017

Incorrect. Variable compensation arising from recommendations made after the applicability date is not permitted by the GE.
Correct. Variable compensation arising from recommendations made after the applicability date is not permitted by the GE.

Recommend, after April 10, 2017, an increase of $250 per month to Ruth’s pre-existing systematic investment plan program

Incorrect. The $250 per month increase is not covered by the GE.
Correct. The $250 per month increase is not covered by the GE.

Recommend changes to Ruth’s pre-existing IRA portfolio as part of a periodic rebalancing strategy established before April 10, 2017 (your compensation will continue at pre-existing commission rates)

Correct. This is permitted because your compensation rate will not increase.
Incorrect. This is permitted because your compensation rate will not increase.

2.

You are a Fiduciary Advisor relying upon the GE. Are you allowed to receive variable compensation from the following?

Upfront commissions resulting from a recommendation made after the applicability date

Incorrect. Upfront commissions earned from a recommendation made after the applicability date are not eligible. The GE does not apply. A BICE is required.
Correct. Upfront commissions earned from a recommendation made after the applicability date are not eligible. The GE does not apply. A BICE is required.

Trailing commissions resulting from a recommendation made after the applicability date

Incorrect. Trailing commissions earned from a recommendation made after the applicability date are not eligible. The GE does not apply. A BICE is required.
Correct. Trailing commissions earned from a recommendation made after the applicability date are not eligible. The GE does not apply. A BICE is required.

12(b)-1 fees resulting from a $2,000 per month systematic investment plan in place before the applicability date and continuing beyond the applicability date

Correct. Variable compensation earned under systematic investment plans in place before the applicability date is eligible for the GE.
Incorrect. Variable compensation earned under systematic investment plans in place before the applicability date is eligible for the GE.

Additional 12(b)-1 fees resulting from a $1,000 increase, after the applicability date, in a systematic investment plan in place before the applicability date

Incorrect. The $1,000 is an increase (after the applicability date) to a systematic investment plan in place before the applicability date and is not eligible for the GE.
Correct. The $1,000 is an increase (after the applicability date) to a systematic investment plan in place before the applicability date and is not eligible for the GE.
