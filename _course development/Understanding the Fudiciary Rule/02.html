What the DOL Fiduciary Rule Means to You

Your legal duty to clients may have skyrocketed and your ability to earn unrestricted compensation may have plummeted as a result of the Fiduciary Rule (The Rule). Advisors subject to The Rule may be considered fiduciary advisors and must act in the best interests of their clients.

Understanding whether or not you are a Fiduciary Advisor and, if so, how that impacts your practice is critical for the very survival, if not the growth, of your business. A Fiduciary Advisor is simply a covered advisor making a covered recommendation. We will explain that deceptively simple definition in the pages that follow.
