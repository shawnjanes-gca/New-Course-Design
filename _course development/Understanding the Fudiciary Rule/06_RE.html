Review Exercise

1.

You are a Covered Advisor in which of the following scenarios?

You acknowledge fiduciary status to a client.

Correct. You are a Covered Advisor whenever you acknowledge fiduciary status.
Incorrect. You are a Covered Advisor whenever you acknowledge fiduciary status.

You provide individual investment advice, pursuant to an agreement (oral or written) or understanding, to a client based upon his or her specific needs.

Correct. You are a Covered Advisor whenever you provide individualized investment advice to meet a client’s specific needs.
Incorrect. You are a Covered Advisor whenever you provide investment advice to meet a client’s specific need.

You provide individual advice to a client based upon his or her specific needs, but you have no contract or understanding to do so.

Correct. You are a Covered Advisor whenever you provide investment advice to meet a client’s specific needs even if you have no agreement or mutual understanding with the client.
Incorrect. You are a Covered Advisor whenever you provide investment advice to meet a client’s specific needs even if you have no agreement or mutual understanding with the client.

2.

Are you making a Covered Recommendation in the following scenarios?

I make investment recommendations to owners of taxable accounts.

Incorrect. Taxable account owners are not considered Retail Retirement Investors.
Correct. Taxable account owners are not considered Retail Retirement Investors.

I conduct purely educational seminars (no investment advice is given) for Retail Retirement Investors.

Incorrect. Purely educational communications are not considered investment recommendations.
Correct. Purely educational communications are not considered investment recommendations.

I provide income tax planning recommendations to Retail Retirement Investors.

Incorrect. Advice unrelated to investment advice is not considered an investment recommendation.
Correct. Advice unrelated to investment advice is not considered an investment recommendation.

I receive compensation as a result of making individualized investment recommendations to Retail Retirement Investors.

Correct. The statement correctly defines a Covered Recommendation.
Incorrect. The statement correctly defines a Covered Recommendation.
