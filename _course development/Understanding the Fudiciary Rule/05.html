The 3 and 3 Fiduciary Advisor

You are a Fiduciary Advisor if you are a Covered Advisor making Covered Recommendations. The rules governing your fiduciary status can be confusing. The following “3 and 3” chart may help you remember how one becomes a Fiduciary Advisor.
