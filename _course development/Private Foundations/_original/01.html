Introduction

Although most often associated with the super-rich, private charitable foundations are an important tool used increasingly by individuals and families of more ordinary wealth. This module reviews the fundamental aspects of private foundations: how they can be used, the benefits they offer, when a private foundation might be considered, and some of the limitations involved.

Objectives

After learning the topics in this module, you will:

 

1. Understand the background of Private Foundations and their purpose as charitable vehicles

2. Have a clear perspective of the types of clients who should consider establishing a Private Foundation

3. Be able to identify situations where creating a Private Foundation might be especially appropriate

4. Be able to identify the steps involved in establishing a Private Foundation

5. Understand the key rules regarding Private Foundations, such as restrictions on investments and contributions

6. Understand the benefits of Private Foundations, especially their tax benefit
