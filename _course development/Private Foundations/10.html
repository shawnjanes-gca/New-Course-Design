Charitable Goals to Consider

Many clients, particularly those who have not previously considered a private foundation, may be relatively unclear in their own minds as to their charitable goals. While this can present something of a challenge, there are several rules of thumb, or key items to look for. The following is a brief list of some of these key items:

Key Charitable Goals

  1. A desire to create a unique and perhaps permanent legacy;
  2. A desire to teach their values and share their vision with children/grandchildren;
  3. A desire to maintain flexibility to adapt charitable activities as the client learns more and as circumstances change;
  4. An interest in maintaining legal control over charitable assets;
  5. The desire to give, yet remain anonymous;
  6. A desire, whether articulated or not, to "make a difference."
