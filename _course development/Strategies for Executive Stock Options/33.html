Conclusion

Equity awards are, and continue to be, a core part of executive compensation. Additionally, many other people receive these awards, such as directors, consultants, and even employees below the executive level. It is important to have a working knowledge of these awards in order to assist your clients with the multiple decisions surrounding them.
