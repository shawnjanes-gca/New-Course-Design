Introduction

While IRAs are not necessarily the primary vehicle for wealth accumulation, many clients today have significant wealth in these tax-deferred accounts as a result of rollovers from qualified plans. It is, therefore, important that financial services professionals be knowledgeable regarding IRA opportunities, strategies, and current regulations.

At the core, the regulations governing IRAs are designed to accomplish two primary objectives:

  • Simplicity in the regulations governing distributions from these vehicles.
  • Flexibility for the participant/IRA owner in making and planning for choices regarding distributions after their death.
