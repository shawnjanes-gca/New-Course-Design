Distributions from an IRA

There are many different situations where distributions from an IRA are required. This module is focused on distributions that are required upon the death of the IRA owner and the planning required for those with significant IRA balances. While relevant in some instances, this module does not deal with the tactics and implications of distributions during the IRA owner's life.
