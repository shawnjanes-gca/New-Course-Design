Objectives

Now more than ever, affluent clients are looking for advisors who are able to help them focus on these key issues to effectively structure and manage their portfolio, simplifying what is all too often a complex process.  This course will equip you with:

  • A practical understanding of the 5 key issues for investment success

  • A framework for helping clients discern what they should be doing in their investment planning.

  • An understanding of investment risks and how to measure and manage them

  • Tools for measuring success in meeting investment plan objectives
