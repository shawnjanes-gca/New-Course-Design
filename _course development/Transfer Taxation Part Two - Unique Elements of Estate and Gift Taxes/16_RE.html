Upon Mrs. Goldsmith's death, indicate which of the following items would be included in Mrs. Goldsmith's estate tax return by choosing the appropriate yes or no response below:

Her bank certificate of deposit that will automatically distribute to her son upon her death.

Incorrect. Since she could change this designation while alive, she had full control of those funds.

Mrs. Goldsmith's life insurance policy in which she named her son as the beneficiary.

Correct. She owned the policy and had the power to name the beneficiary, therefore it is included in her estate even though the proceeds are payable to her son.
Incorrect. She owned the policy and had the power to name the beneficiary, therefore it is included in her estate even though the proceeds are payable to her son.

Insurance on her life that was purchased by her son naming himself as the beneficiary.

Incorrect. She was not the owner of the policy, therefore the proceeds are not part of her estate.
Correct. She was not the owner of the policy, therefore the proceeds are not part of her estate.

Mrs. Goldsmith's life insurance policy that she transferred to an irrevocable trust five years ago

Incorrect. The policy would be included in her estate only if she made the transfer less than three years ago.
Correct. The policy would be included in her estate only if she made the transfer less than three years ago.

Her personal property

Correct. Her personal property is part of her estate.
Incorrect. Her personal property is part of her estate.

Her funeral expenses

Incorrect. While funeral expenses can be deducted from the estate, they are not themselves part of the estate upon which taxes are calculated.
Correct. While funeral expenses can be deducted from the estate, they are not themselves part of the estate upon which taxes are calculated.

The rental property she transferred to her son, where she retained the right to the rental income

Correct. This is an incomplete transfer and it is brought back into her estate.
Incorrect. This is an incomplete transfer and it is brought back into her estate.

Her annuity that she purchased two years ago that pays out to her son upon her death

Correct, since she purchased the annuity.
Incorrect, since she purchased the annuity.

The annuity that her son purchased to provide her with income and which pays a death benefit to her son upon her death

Incorrect, because the annuity was purchased by her son.
Correct, because her son purchased the annuity.
