Summary Review of Transfer Taxes

This concludes the three-part overview of transfer taxation relevant to estate planning. As a review, the chart below puts together the preceding changes regarding transfer taxation into a single chart. Here are the most important points to remember:

  • The Applicable Exclusion Amount, GST Tax Exemption Amount, and Gift Tax Exclusion Amount are all $5,450,000 in 2016.
  • The maximum rate for gift and estate taxes is 40% in 2016.
  • The GST Tax rate, a flat tax at the maximum rate on the Unified Transfer Tax Rate Schedule, is 40% in 2016.
