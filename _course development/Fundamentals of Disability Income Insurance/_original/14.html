The 4-Step Process to Determine the Need for Disability Income Insurance - Step 2

Step 2: Determine current post-disability sources of income

The need for post-disability income can be offset with any income available from sources such as Social Security, existing insurance, and investment income. By subtracting these resources from the needs established in Step 1, the design and amount of disability insurance required can be determined.
