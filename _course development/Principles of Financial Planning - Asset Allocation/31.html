Establishing an "Efficient Frontier"

With MVO, we can input expected return, standard deviation, and correlation of multiple assets (asset classes) and examine the possible risk return profiles of various allocations among those assets (asset classes). Since the possible combinations are extensive, the outcome is best captured graphically. The presentation below shows the risk profiles of various combinations of assets by plotting their risk (as measured by standard deviation) on the X-axis and expected return on the Y-axis; it also introduces the concept of the "Efficient Frontier." The Efficient Frontier defines the historically highest returns an investor could generate at any given level of risk (defined as standard deviation, which is often represented by the Greek letter "Ïƒ") over a specified time frame.
